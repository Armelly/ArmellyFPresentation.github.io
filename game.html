Point Rush

×

Objective

Be the first player to reach 100 points or have the highest score at the end of the set rounds.


Set up

Players decide how many rounds they will play.


Gameplay

Decide the order of play.

On a player's turn, they roll the die and tally up points with each roll.

The player can roll as many times as they wish on a single turn, accumulating points. However, if they roll a one, they lose all points from that turn, and their turn ends.

If a player rolls multiple sixes, they can choose to "hold" and end their turn, securing the points earned so far.

Once a player decides to hold, the die passes to the next player, and the cycle repeats.
Play continues in this manner until a player reaches 100 points or until the set number of rounds is completed.

If a player reaches 100 points, they are the winner. Alternatively, if all rounds are completed, the player with the highest score wins.
